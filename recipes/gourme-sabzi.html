Gourme Sabzi

Description:

Ghormeh Sabzi (Persian: قورمه‌ سبزی ) is an Iranian or Persian dish from antiquity. It smells good, tastes better and is nourishing on top of that. Usually or rather always served with cooked white rice in Iran, this dish has a special place in every Iranian's heart, including this writer's :) If there is one food that any foreigner wishes to taste and get the gist of Persian cuisine, it's this one. It is THE signature dish of Iran.

Ingredients:

  • 0.5kg red meat
  • 250cc beans
  • 250 grams Vegetables
  • 2 dried limes
  • 1 onion
  • salt
  • pepper
  • turmeric

Steps:

  1. Slice onions into tiny bits, and then cook them with sunflower oil i.e. fry them.
  2. Add the red meat in chunks to the pan in which you're frying you onions and fry the meat too.
  3. While you're frying the fuck outta onions and red meat slices, add salt, pepper, and turmeric 'cause why not.
  4. Do not stop frying the mixtures and add the pre-fried Vegetables to the pan.
  5. Add about 1000cc water to the mixture and let it sit for about 2 to 3 hours with a very mild fire.
  6. When it's almost ready, add the dried limes and let it sit for another 30 minutes.
  7. After 30 minutes have passed, take the pan off the heat. It's readyyyyyy.
